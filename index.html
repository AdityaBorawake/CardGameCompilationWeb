Rules

How to play

Sevens

3-player board-building race, full 52-card deck. Holder of 7♥ goes first and must open with it.

  • Place one card per turn — rank must be exactly one step beyond the lane's current edge.
  • Open a lane with its 7, then extend up toward A or down toward 2.
  • No legal card? Turn is skipped automatically.
  • First player to empty their hand wins.

Five-Three-Two

3-player trick-taking, 30-card deck (6 to A, all suits, 10 cards each). The quota-5 player picks trump each round.

  • Rotating quotas: 5 tricks, 3 tricks, 2 tricks — one per player per round.
  • The quota-5 player sees their first 5 cards and names trump — or reveals 2 extra cards and picks from those suits.
  • Follow the led suit if possible; otherwise play any card.
  • Highest trump wins the trick. No trump? Highest of the led suit wins.
  • Hit quota exactly to score 1 point. No one exact? Closest scores instead.
  • First to 3 points wins the match.

Beggar-Moneylender

Fast 2-player pile-capture, full 52 cards. Each player: 4-card hand + 22-card reserve.

  • Flip the top card of your hand onto the central pile.
  • If the rank matches any card already in the pile, capture the whole pile into your reserve.
  • Hand refills to 4 after each flip.
  • Empty hand and reserve = eliminated. Last one with cards wins.
